## Publications

1. **Microscopic mechanisms of magnetoelectric coupling**  
   *Physical Review B*  
   Short abstract: A symmetry-resolved theory identifies dominant spin-lattice channels responsible for electric polarization in noncollinear magnets.  
   arXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.01234

2. **Topological transport in low-symmetry crystals**  
   *Physical Review Letters*  
   Short abstract: We show how Berry curvature hot spots in low-symmetry bands produce anisotropic Hall-like responses without conventional ferromagnetism.  
   arXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.04567

3. **Data-driven discovery of quantum phase boundaries**  
   *Journal of Chemical Physics*  
   Short abstract: A hybrid ML + theory framework maps phase transitions from sparse simulation data while preserving physically meaningful order parameters.  
   arXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.07890
